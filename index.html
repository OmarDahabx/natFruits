About organic food

Organic food refers to food produced using methods that promote environmental sustainability and reduce the use of synthetic chemicals and artificial fertilizers. Organic food is considered a healthy and preferred choice by many people due to its numerous benefits. Organic foods contain higher levels of nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They are also free from pesticides and harmful chemicals that can accumulate in other types of food.
